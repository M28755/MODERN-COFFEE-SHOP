Our Journey

Crafted Over Time

2022

The First Roast

It started in a small garage with a single 1-kilogram roaster. We weren't trying to build an empire; we were just trying to find the perfect cup. Hours were spent chasing that elusive sound—the first crack of a perfectly roasted bean.

Opening the Doors

What began as weekend pop-ups turned into our first brick-and-mortar home. We designed Aura to be a sanctuary—warm lighting, tactile surfaces, and the constant, comforting hum of the espresso machine. A place to slow down.

A Growing Family

Today, Aura is more than a coffee shop; it's a community of slow-living advocates. We source directly from farms that share our ethos, ensuring every cup poured is a testament to patience, craft, and connection.
